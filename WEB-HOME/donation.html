NUTRITION

1. Healthy Cooked Food for Underprivileged Children: One key component of the Annapurna Mission is the provision of nutritious cooked meals to underprivileged children. This addresses the immediate needs of children who are often most vulnerable to malnutrition. By ensuring they receive regular, healthy meals, the Mission aims to improve their overall health and well-being. 2. Dry Ration Kits for Visually Impaired Individuals: The Annapurna Mission recognizes that visually impaired individuals face unique challenges in accessing food and nutrition. To support them, the Mission distributes dry ration kits containing essential food items that are easy to handle and prepare. This assistance empowers visually impaired individuals to maintain their nutritional needs independently.

EDUCATION

Mission Vidhya focuses on providing quality education to underprivileged children and youth. The mission supports school dropouts, visually impaired students, and children from low-income families by providing books, stationery, school fees, and scholarships. Through education, we empower the next generation to break the cycle of poverty and build a better future for themselves and their communities.

ATMA NIRBHAR

Mission Aadhar's Atma Nirbhar initiative focuses on making individuals self-reliant through skill development and vocational training. We provide livelihood support to visually impaired individuals, widows, and underprivileged youth by equipping them with practical skills — tailoring, computer training, handicrafts, and more — enabling them to earn independently and live with dignity.

WOMEN EMPOWERMENT

Mission Aurat is dedicated to empowering women from underprivileged backgrounds through education, skill development, legal awareness, and health support. We work towards building a society where every woman can stand independently, raise her voice against injustice, and contribute meaningfully to her family and community. Your donation helps us run women's self-help groups, vocational training programs, and health camps.
