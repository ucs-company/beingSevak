Provide essential livelihood toolkits to underprivileged individuals — carpentry sets, tailoring kits, plumbing tools, and more. Rojgar Booth

Support our employment assistance booths that connect daily-wage workers, labourers, and job seekers with local employers. Rojgar Booths provide a platform where people can register their skills, find work opportunities, and achieve financial independence. Each booth serves as a bridge between the unemployed and meaningful work.

Empower women and differently-abled individuals with sewing machines to start their own tailoring business. A sewing machine is not just a tool — it is a source of income, confidence, and independence. We distribute high-quality machines along with initial training and fabric to help recipients begin earning immediately.

Floor Meal Support

Provide nutritious floor meals to daily-wage workers, homeless individuals, and families who cannot afford a proper meal. Our floor meal programme serves hot, hygienic food at shelters, construction sites, and slum communities. Every meal brings hope and strength to those struggling to survive.

Tricycle (Wheelchair) Distribution

Distribute specially designed tricycles and wheelchairs to elderly and differently-abled individuals who cannot walk. A tricycle restores mobility, freedom, and the ability to perform daily tasks independently. Each tricycle is customised to the user's needs and comes with a free maintenance checkup.

Blind Stick Distribution

Provide high-quality white canes (blind sticks) to visually impaired individuals to help them navigate independently. A sturdy, reflective cane is a lifeline for the blind — enabling safe movement, building confidence, and reducing dependency on others. We distribute ergonomic canes with free mobility training.
