ABOUT US

Being Sevak Charitable Trust represents as National Nonprofit Organizations working since 2015 as a servant of society. Founded by a team of social workers who are working in the field of health, education, vocational training, empowering women and child development. As the name Being Sevak suggests from the name "SEVAK BANO" is committed to serving selfless service for the society.

Being Sevak Charitable Trust support different low income groups of people like Visually Impaired Individual, underprivileged children & needy individual in India. NGO has always been extending hands to help all these needy people who dwell out of ashrams & face the crisis to meet their basic needs of life. With the support of people like you, we are able to help many of these families and individuals not only to meet their essential daily needs, but also to manage their livelihood through our Mission Like ANNAPURNA, VIDHYA, BEACH SEVAKS, AURAT and many other activities. Registered as not for profit organization in 2015 under the Bombay Trust Act. BSCT Registration No: E-31948/Mumbai/2014-15.
