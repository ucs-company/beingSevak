Management Team

Meet the dedicated leaders of Being Sevak Charitable Trust who are passionately working towards social welfare, empowerment, and inclusive growth for society.

Priyank Shah
Trustee

Trustee Mr. Priyank Shah, Trustee of the Being Sevak Charitable Trust & the eminent CEO of Eatonik Industries, renowned for the widely acclaimed Kanchan Mouth Freshener brand. His life's motto, "Sevak Bano," reflects his deep commitment to selfless service and social welfare.

With over 15 years of experience in social work, he has actively collaborated with multiple NGOs and worked extensively for specially-abled individuals. Inspired by his mother's dedication to social service, Mr. Shah continues to champion empowerment, inclusivity, and compassionate leadership for a better society.

Shweta Shah
Secretary

Founder Mrs. Shweta Shah is a seasoned social worker with over 11 years of experience dedicated to serving visually impaired individuals, underprivileged children, and people in need.

Her diligent, devoted, and compassionate nature has helped uplift countless lives through various welfare initiatives. As Secretary of Being Sevak Charitable Trust and a consulting social worker, she has played a vital role in supporting organizations working for the visually impaired and the needy.

Her tireless contribution and unwavering dedication continue to elevate the mission and impact of Being Sevak Charitable Trust across communities.
