Founder's Note

Life has always been a journey filled with ups and downs. There was a time when every project started by me and my team became a great success. But life teaches us that every challenge comes with a new beginning. Due to some unavoidable circumstances, I made the difficult decision to resign from my job.

However, I strongly believe that when your intentions are pure and your passion is genuine, life always opens another door. After every storm, there is a sunrise — and for me, that sunrise came through the unwavering support and belief of my team, which inspired the Organisation of Being Sevak Charitable Trust.

I believe that even two helping hands can create wonders in society. With one simple mantra — "Sevak Bano" — our mission is to spread kindness, dignity, empowerment, and hope among underprivileged, visually impaired, specially-abled, and needy individuals.

Together, we are not just serving people; we are changing lives.

Priyank Shah
