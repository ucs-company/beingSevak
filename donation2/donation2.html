WHAT WE DO

Our Projects

Click on a mission to learn more and donate directly.

Mission Annapurna

Providing nutritious meals and dry ration kits to underprivileged children and visually impaired individuals across Mumbai.

Mission Vidya

Quality education support for underprivileged children, school dropouts, and visually impaired students through scholarships and supplies.

Mission Aurat

Empowering women through education, skill development, legal awareness, health support, and self-help groups.

Mission Bezubaan

Animal welfare initiatives including rescue, medical care, feeding programs, and shelter for stray and abandoned animals.

Mission Atmanirbhar

Skill development and vocational training for visually impaired individuals, widows, and underprivileged youth to achieve self-reliance.

Mission Arogya

Healthcare initiatives including medical camps, eye check-ups, health awareness drives, and medicine distribution for the needy.

Mission Eco Warriors

Environmental conservation projects including tree plantation drives, beach clean-ups, recycling awareness, and sustainability education.
